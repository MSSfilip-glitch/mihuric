# PADI Tec courses

If you are ready to transition from recreational to technical diving then you will need to complete technical diving courses. We offer **the full PADI Tec Diver program** during which you will practice full technical deep decompression diving.

To enrol in any of the Tec courses you have to be **at least 18 years old** and bring **a medical certificate** signed by your physician in the past 12 months.

# Tec 40

**Prerequisites:** PADI AOWD certification (or equivalent), PADI Enriched Air Diver  certification (or equivalent), at least 30 logged dives with a minimum of 10 dives to 30 meters / 100 feet (or a PADI Deep Diver  certification / equivalent from a different diving association) and another 10 dives using enriched air deeper than 18 meters / 60 feet

**Duration:**

**Description:** You will gain experience and knowledge for technical diving. With this certification you are qualified to make limited decompression dives to 40 meters / 130 feet.

**Further education:** Rescue Diver, Tec 45

# Tec 45

**Prerequisites:** PADI Tec 40 certification (or equivalent), PADI Rescue Diver certification (or equivalent), at least 50 logged dives, with at least 12 dives on enriched air nitrox deeper than 18 meters / 60 feet and 6 dives deeper than 30 meters / 100 feet.

**Duration:**

**Description:** This is the second part of the program in which you will extend your depth limit to 45 meters / 145 feet. You’ll learn to plan and complete repetitive decompression dives using a single stage. You will also make accelerated decompression dives using EANx or pure oxygen.

**Further education:** Tec 50

# Tec 50

**Prerequisites:** PADI Tec 45 certification (or equivalent), at least 100 logged dives, with at least 20 being on enriched air nitrox deeper than 18 meters / 60 feet and 15 dives deeper than 30 meters / 100 feet.

**Duration:**

**Description:** This is the third part of the program in which you will extend your depth limit to 50 meters / 165 feet. You will learn how to make extended, accelerated decompression stops using two stages. This certification opens the door to deeper diving expeditions.

**Further education:** [Tec Trimix 65](https://www.padi.com/courses/tec-trimix-65)
