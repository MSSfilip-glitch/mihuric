# Discover a new world with us

Are you thinking about embarking on your first underwater adventure? Or are you thinking about getting back into it after a break? Either way, we like how you think J Scuba diving is so much fun, we cannot oversell it.

Whether you are brand new or making a return to diving, you probably have questions about the cost, duration, safety… On this page you can find all the necessary information.

We assure you that with us, you are in safe hands and we hope to see you soon at diving center Mihurić.

# Discover Scuba Diving (DSD)

**Maximum depth**: 5 meters / 16 feet with a DSD leader, 12 meters / 40 feet with a PADI instructor

**Minimum age**: 10

**Duration**: 1.5 hours

**Description**: An introduction dive for anyone who wants to try out the exciting sport of scuba diving.

**Further education**: PADI Open Water Diver course

# Bubblemaker

**Maximum depth**: 2 meters / 6 feet

**Minimum age**: 8

**Duration**: 1 hour

**Description**: A discovery dive specially designed for kids who aren’t old enough for the regular DSD.

# Check dive

**Maximum depth**: 5 meters / 16 feet with a DSD leader, 12 meters / 40 feet with a PADI instructor

**Minimum age**: 10

**Duration**: 1.5 hours

**Description**: A dive to refresh your knowledge and skills. If you don’t dive regularly and tend to have bigger breaks between your dives, it is wise to do a check dive before you continue.

## Here are some things to consider before you book:

### Duration

You will only need to free up **a couple of hours** **for a DSD, Bubblemaker or Scuba review**, but if you decide you love it and want to continue, you will need at least another **4 days for the PADI OWD course**  or **2 days for the PADI Scuba diver course.**

### Cost

The cost of scuba diving is comparable to other adventure sports. **For an exact price** of your chosen diving program, please **contact us** (link to form).

### Equipment

If you are a beginner, you probably don’t have your own equipment. This is fine, as we have a wide range of **equipment for rent** **(included in the price of the DSD, Bubblemaker and Scuba review)** and we will find the gear that suits you best.

### Safety

A common concern among new divers – is scuba diving safe? We take the wellbeing of our divers very seriously. Our staff is highly professional and will **ensure you feel safe and comfortable.**

### Medical certificate

A medical certificate signed by your physician is **only necessary if you have any health issues.** If you are healthy, then you will only need to sign a medical statement at the diving center.

### Environment

As scuba divers we have a responsibility to protect the ocean. The first step is to be considerate of your surroundings. Remember, you are a guest in the underwater world. The golden rule is: **Take only photos, leave only bubbles.**
