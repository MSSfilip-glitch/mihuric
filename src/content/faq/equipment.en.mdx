# Equipment FAQ

Is equipment rental included in the price of the courses?

Equipment rental is included in the price of the DSD, Bubblemaker, Scuba review and Open Water Diver and Scuba Diver courses. That’s how we lure you in. Once you’re hooked on diving and you’re ready to do more courses, it costs extra.
