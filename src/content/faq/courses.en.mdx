# Courses FAQ

What is the right diving course for me?

The course you choose depends on your level of experience. Check out the PADI [recreational](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-recreational-courses/), [specialty](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-specialty-courses/)  and [professional](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-pro/) courses we offer to see which one is suitable for you. If you have never tried diving before, then you should do a [discovery dive](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/discovery-dives/) first. If you still can’t find what you’re looking for or if you’re ready to book, [contact us](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/sign-up/).

What is PADI eLearning?

PADI eLearning is an online system that offers divers the possibilities to get learning materials online or even do some of the theoretical exams online. It’s super convenient! Think about it, you can study the materials wherever and whenever you want. On the couch, in your bed, on the toilet… Whatever, we’re not judging. Click [here](https://shop.padi.com/catalog?lang=en&irra=9664&affiliate_id=9664) to see which materials are available.

Are there any age restrictions for doing a course?

Yes. Kiddos have to be at least 8 just to try diving and 10 to do a course.  More specifically, candidates for the Open Water Diver, Scuba Diver and Adventure Diver course need to be at least 10 years old, while candidates for other [recreational courses](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-recreational-courses/)  need to be at least 12. The minimum age for [specialty courses](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-specialty-courses/) depends on the course. Candidates for [professional courses](http://mihu.hostspot.com.hr/padi-pro/) need to be 18.

On the plus side, there is no maximum age for diving. As long as you are healthy you can keep diving forever 🙂

Can I do a PADI course if I am certified at another diving association?

Yes, we welcome infidels, especially those who want to convert 🙂

Seriously though, if you are transferring to PADI from another diving association, you will need to complete a Crossover program. This way you don’t have to start from the beginning, but can continue at the same level as in the other association.

When do I get my diving certification?

Are you still here? You haven’t even booked a course yet and you’re asking about the certification?

Alright, here’s the deal. Once you pass your chosen course you will get a confirmation from us and we will register you as a certified diver with PADI, so you can start diving immediately. It will take a few weeks for the actual certification to arrive, but it will be delivered to your home address.
