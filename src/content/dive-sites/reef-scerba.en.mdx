# Reef Šćerba

**Min. depth**: 12 m / 40 feet

**Max. depth**: 50 m / 165 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The reef starts out as two rocky pillars which connect at around 20 meters below surface. Advanced divers descend along a wall densely covered with interesting flora, while beginners remain in shallower waters between rock formations, where they can explore a rich underwater world.
