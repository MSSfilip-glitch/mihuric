# Shipwreck SS Regolo

**Min. depth:** 50 m /165 feet

**Max. depth:** 66 m / 215 feet

**Certification:** Tec Trimix 60 (or equivalent)

**Description:** The cargo steamship went missing from the harbor in Senj on a stormy night in February 1956. None of the 13 crew members survived and the location of the shipwreck remained a mystery until the summer of 2017. Its discovery has piqued the interest of many divers. However, because it lies so deep, only TEC divers get to explore this wreck.
