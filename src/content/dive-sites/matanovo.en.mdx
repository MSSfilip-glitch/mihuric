# Matanovo

**Min. depth**: 2 m / 6 feet

**Max. depth**: 25 m / 82 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The walls rise from 30 meters deep all the way up to the surface. Although at first glance they appear separated, they are connected just a few meters below surface, forming a saddle that is home to slugs and octopuses. Smaller red gorgonians reside on the south side, while the whole location is rich with sponges.
