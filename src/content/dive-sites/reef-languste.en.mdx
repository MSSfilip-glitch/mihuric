# Reef Languste

**Min. depth**: 25 m / 82 feet

**Max. depth**: 45 m / 145 feet

**Certification**: AOWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: This location is home to many lobsters, which prevail in the deeper parts of the reef at around 40 m. Lobsters are not the only red things here. Every diver will surely enjoy the vast fields of red gorgonians.
