# Timo’s caves

**Min. depth**: 5 m / 16 feet

**Max. depth**: 25 m / 82 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The biggest attraction is the string of caves and caverns at only 5 meters. They are wide enough for multiple divers and the rocky bottom ensures that the visibility stays good. There are many shells on the wall thanks to the flow of freshwater. It is possible to ascend in the caverns and enjoy the beautiful cave ceilings or even take a walk to the next cavern instead of diving to it… just to mix things up 🙂
