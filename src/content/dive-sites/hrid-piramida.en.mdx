**Min. depth**: 3 m / 10 feet

**Max. depth**: 27 m / 88 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: A simple, yet attractive location for all divers. We dive around the rock and it doesn’t matter where you decide to ascend, because of the shape of the rock, you will always come up by the boat.
