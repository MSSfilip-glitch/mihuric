# Reef Katedrala

**Min. depth**: 27 m / 88 feet

**Max. depth**: 40 m / 130 feet

**Certification**: AOWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: Divers descend along a rope to two cone shaped reefs, which are rich with large fish, such as groupers and dentex. It is easy to lose track of time at this interesting location, but it is important to try and avoid decompression. However, if you do have to hang at the decompression stop for a few minutes, you won’t be bored with all the fish keeping you company.
