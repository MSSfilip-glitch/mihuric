# St. Marak

**Min. depth**: 5 m / 16 feet

**Max. depth**: 30 m / 100 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The dive starts at a plateau at 5 meters, followed by a steep descent along the wall. One of the highlights is a small wreck at the entrance of a cave that can be entered one diver at a time. The cave is at 15 meters, but it is possible to descend further down the wall.
