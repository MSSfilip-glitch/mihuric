# OW Reef Druženjin

**Min. depth**: 10 m / 33 feet

**Max. depth**: 25 m / 82 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: Beginners can practice diving along different types of seabed, while experienced divers, especially underwater photographers, can take advantage of the presence of at least 10 sorts of slugs, as well as conger eels and seahorses. One of the highlights is the rare Elkhorn coral.
