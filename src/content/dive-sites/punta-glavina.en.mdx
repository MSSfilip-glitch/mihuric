# Punta Glavina

**Min. depth**: 3 m / 10 feet

**Max. depth**: 45 m / 145 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: A beautiful wall descends gradually to 25 meters below the surface. A few fin strokes away is the edge of a second wall that reaches all the way down to 45 meters. The location is suitable for divers of different levels of experience. One of the highlights of this location is a large cave, with an entrance at only 3 meters.
