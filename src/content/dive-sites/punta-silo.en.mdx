# Punta Šilo

**Min. depth**: 6 m / 19 feet

**Max depth**: 28 m / 91 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: At the very beginning there is a wall descending to 14 meters below sea level, followed by a 20 meter long sand plateau and again a wall that extends to 28 meters. The location is rich with fish, octopuses and corals.
