# Plovanov Stol

**Min. depth**: 10 m / 33 feet

**Max. depth**: 45 m / 145 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: In a vertical drop to the depth of 45 meters the wall offers a colorful palette of underwater life, including fish, corals, sponges and more. The most attractive part of the wall is between 25 and 40 meters. At the end of the dive, if you flash your light into the half meter wide vertical crack in the wall, you might be surprised by the number and variety of fish that hides here.
