# House reef

**Min. depth**: 2 m / 6 feet

**Max. depth**: 35 m / 115 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: Ideal for novice, as well as experienced divers. Our house reef offers a number of underwater ‘’attractions’’, such as a tribute to the Roman god of the ocean Neptune, smaller wrecks, a 2 m tall anchor, various fish, octopuses, slugs, sea horses…
