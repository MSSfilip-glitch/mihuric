# Hrid Kamenjak

**Min. depth**: 2 m / 6 feet

**Max. depth**: 35 m / 115 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: This location is double the fun, because it offers not one, but two reefs. The first one goes down to 25 meters and is rich with slugs, sponges and yellow gorgonians. The second reef extends down to 35 meters and offers endemic sponges.
