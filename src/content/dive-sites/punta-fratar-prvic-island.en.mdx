# Punta Fratar, Prvić island

**Min. depth**: 10 m / 33 feet

**Max. depth**: 50 m / 165 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The outer side of the wall can seem relatively empty, but don’t be fooled, because the cracks hide a variety of slugs and crabs. Be sure to flash your light and check it out before moving on to the next part. The inner side of the wall is densely covered with yellow and red gorgonians. The colorful transition from red gorgonians to the white sandy seabed to the deep blue sea is quite attractive.
