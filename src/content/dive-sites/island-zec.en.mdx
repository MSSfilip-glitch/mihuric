# Island Zec

**Min. depth**: 10 m / 33 feet

**Max. depth**: 45 m / 145 feet

**Certification**: OWD (or equivalent)

**Description**: The shallower parts are attractive due to the presence of octopuses, crabs and smaller fish, while advanced divers will enjoy big red gorgonians and fish, such as the dentex, grouper and conger eel.
